Welcome to Islamabad Unravelled!

We're a team of passionate travel enthusiasts dedicated to helping budget-conscious tourists explore the hidden gems of Islamabad. We believe everyone deserves to experience the rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning beauty of Islamabad, regardless of budget limitations.

Introducing our Mobile App:

Islamabad Unravelled is your one-stop shop for planning an unforgettable and affordable trip to Islamabad. Our user-friendly mobile app empowers you to:

  • Discover hidden gems: Explore detailed descriptions of Islamabad's top tourist attractions, historical sites, museums, and cultural landmarks. Get estimated costs for entry fees, tours, and transportation so you can plan your budget effectively.
  • Navigate with ease: Utilize our interactive maps with location services to find your way around Islamabad and discover nearby points of interest. Never get lost again!
  • Stay on budget: Set a daily or total trip budget and utilize our unique filtering system to find destinations, attractions, restaurants, and accommodation options that fit your spending plan. No more unexpected expenses!
  • Find the perfect stay & travel: Browse listings and booking options for hotels, hostels, and transportation services in Islamabad, with clear pricing information to help you make informed decisions.
  • Join the travel community: Share your experiences and leave reviews and ratings for Islamabad's destinations and services. Help fellow travelers discover the best spots in the city.

Our Mission:

We believe in promoting responsible tourism and fostering a community of informed travelers. Through user-generated reviews and comprehensive information, Islamabad Unravelled empowers travellers to make informed decisions and have a fulfilling experience in Islamabad. We also aim to contribute to the growth of Islamabad's tourism industry by providing a valuable resource for budget-conscious visitors.
